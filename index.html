WELCOME TO KERALA ACADEMY OF PHARMACY

KAP is beautifully located in Kattakada, Trivandrum, which was previously known as the soul of Kerala, India. It has a well-designed campus with modern amenities and facilities, faculty who have proven themselves in their respective fields by providing an excellent blend of rigor and relevance in their teaching, and staff who are always ready to reach out. KAP has robust industry interactions, research aimed at solving real-world problems, and an exemplary track record in career counseling and placement facilitation. All of these combine to provide a rare synergy that transcends artificial barriers and enables students to follow their hearts with passion and confidence. The mission of KAP is to equip students with pharmacy healthcare knowledge & skills, foster global competencies, inculcate value systems among learners, and provide excellent pharmacy health education & service systems for community development. The main pinnacles of KAP include International Study Tours for Toppers, Industrial Visits, Business English Programs, Extensive Training for placements, and ALP for students' skills enhancement.
