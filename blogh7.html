How to become a Pharmacist

September 14, 2021

In India If you want to become a Pharmacist you need to study D-Pharm (Diplorna in Pharmacy). The Eligibility for getting admissions to D Pharm is A Pass in 12th STD with Physics Chemistry Biology or Mathematics. The Major Subjects in 12th is Physics and Chemistry with any of the Subjects Combinations
D Pharmacy Course some people termed as D Pharm also
The D-Pharm Course Duration is 2 Year where you need to study theory as well as Practical
The Exam Pattern for D-Pharm is Annual and D-Pharmacy is not a very tough Program if the student is really interested to become the Pharmacist
Once the Student is Completed the D Pharmacy course from India need to do the Registration in the State Pharmacy Council for the Pharmacy Council Registration Certificate and By using this State Pharmacy Council Registration Certificate Students can apply for the License for starting the Pharmacy (Medical Store) with Drug Control Department. Drug Control Department is the Govt Authority who takes care about the functions related to Medicine
D-Pharmacy is a the easiest, attractive, Job oriented and relevant course for a student to make his career. As once the student completes the D Pharmacy Course he can start earning at-least 15000 to 25000 Per month as a Salary. Once you become the Pharmacist you are always the Pharmacist. Pharmacist Job is a very respectful in our society D-Pharm Course is a very good option for Girls and Boys but as it will give more power to the girls students as they have a career in the hand which they can use for ever
Students Who Complete the D Pharmacy will get the job easily as a pharmacist as there is good demand in our country for opening of Medical Storms as well as the Pharmacy of Private and Govt Hospitals. D-Pharm Holders can apply Jobs in Private and Govt Sectors
Pharmacy Can be in Open in anywhere in india once the student is become a registered pharmacist. As the Demand of the Medicine usage in the increasing level and So much of importance is there for vaccination in the country so the the pharmacists are having always the role in the Healthcare
Do you know what are area's a D Pharmacy Holder or a Registered Pharmacist Can get Jobs?

  • Pharmacist in Govt or Private Hospitals

  • Pharmacy Quality Analyst

  • Quality Controller

  • Lab Technicians - Pharmacy Colleges

  • Pharmacy Distributor

  • Medical Representative

  • Own Medical Store


There are Good Opportunities if the D Pharm Holders can look in to the Higher Studies as Once the students complete the D Pharm they can join to B Pharmacy through Lateral Entry Option at present they can join to 2nd Year of B Pharmacy Program and Complete the course in 3 Year and After the Completion of B-Pharmacy Can Work in the Teacher for D Pharmacy Colleges
